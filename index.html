Hello, World!

I am an applied microeconomist. My research interests lie in Economics of Education, Market Design, and Computational Methods.

My recent research focuses on educational matching markets, such as teacher assignment in Italy and school choice in England.

In my work I like integrating theoretical insights with empirical methods drawn from applied microeconomics, structural econometrics, and machine learning to provide insights into how policies can be structured to create fair and efficient markets.
